## Notes

Leg day is non-negotiable. Squats are king — go deep, stay tight, drive through your heels. RDLs follow for posterior chain balance.

## Tips

- Warm up thoroughly: 5 min bike + 2 light squat sets
- Wear a belt on heavy squats if needed
- Don't skip calves — they need high volume
