## Notes

Start the week strong with heavy pressing. Flat bench is the priority — put your best energy here. Move to incline for upper chest development, then finish with isolation work.

## Tips

- Warm up with 2 light sets of bench press before working sets
- Keep shoulder blades pinched and retracted on all presses
- Control the negative — 2-3 seconds on the way down
