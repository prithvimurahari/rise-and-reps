## Notes

Hit all three delt heads for balanced shoulders. Overhead press builds the foundation, laterals and rear delts add the width and roundness.

## Tips

- Don't ego lift on lateral raises — light weight, strict form
- Keep core braced during overhead press
- Squeeze hard at the top of shrugs — no rolling
