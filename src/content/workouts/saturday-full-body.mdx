## Notes

Full body day hits everything one more time before rest day. Deadlift is the centerpiece — heavy, controlled, powerful. Keep the rest of the session moderate intensity.

## Tips

- Deadlift form is everything — don't round your back
- Chin-ups complement the pressing movements
- Sunday is rest — recover hard, eat well, sleep deep
